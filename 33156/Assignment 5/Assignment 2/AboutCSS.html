Alumni Network




PICT Alumni Network is an initiative by the faculties and departments of Pune Institute of Computer Technology to bring together the the alumni of the instituion and bridge the gap between the students of the institute and the alumni. The main goal of its inception is to create job oppportunities and bring better employment and internships to improve the quality of graduates that the institute produces. We hope you will have a great time here, connecting, growing and networking.
